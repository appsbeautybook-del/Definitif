# Design System: BeautyBook
**Project ID:** 3710180964754178164


## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The application exudes a modern, elegant, and dynamic vibe. The "Light & Vibrant" aesthetic is achieved through a clean white foundation energized by bold orange accents. The interface is airy with generous whitespace, ensuring a premium and homogeneous user experience.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** Primary background and surface color, providing a clean and luminous base.
* **Vibrant Orange (#F48C25):** Primary accent color, used for active navigation icons, buttons, highlights, and promotional elements.
* **Deep Gray (#1F2937):** Primary text color for high legibility on white backgrounds.
* **Medium Gray (#6B7280):** Secondary text and inactive icon color.
* **Light Gray (#F3F4F6):** Subtle borders and secondary backgrounds.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Modern and elegant)
* **Headings:** Bold weights with generous tracking for a premium feel.
* **Body:** Medium and Regular weights for clear readability.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Bottom Navigation Menu:** Fixed at the bottom. 5 icons (Accueil, Services, Assistant IA, Rendez-vous, Profil). Active state in Vibrant Orange.
* **Top Action Menu:** Fixed at the top. 6 icons (Directs, Recherche, Réseau social, Boutique, Immobilier, Fidélité). Consistent styling across all screens.
* **Buttons:** Generously rounded (Pill-shaped or 12px), Vibrant Orange background with white text for primary actions.
* **Cards:** Subtly rounded (8px), white background with whisper-soft diffused shadows for elevation.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Fixed Navigation:** Both Top and Bottom menus are present and identical on every screen.
* **Vertical Organization:** Main content scrolls vertically between the fixed menus.
* **Consistency:** Strict adherence to structural homogeneity to ensure a fluid and intuitive experience.
